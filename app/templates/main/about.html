Our Story

  • Elite Founded with the aim of providing distinctive designs that combine elegance and quality. Behind this creative brand is Mai Ziada, who has extensive experience in the business field spanning over 10 years. Mai began her career in a global company where she held a senior position, but after years of professional success, she chose to leave this position to pursue her deep passion in the world of accessories.
  • More than 15 years ago, Mai began designing and manufacturing accessories herself, and was able to develop her skills and achieve her own creativity in this field. After a period of being away from her dream, Mai returned to achieve what she loves with passion and interest, and established "Elite" as a brand that focuses on excellence and uniqueness.
  • The "Elite" brand seeks to provide innovative designs of the highest quality, while ensuring the use of luxurious materials to meet the needs of lovers of elegance and beauty. By providing products characterized by originality and uniqueness, "Elite" guarantees its customers a luxurious experience at prices that suit everyone.
  • Elite is not just a brand, it is an embodiment of Mai Ziada's love and passion for the world of acessories, with a constant promise of innovation and distinction in every piece she presents.
